Update rule

Source-decomposed Bayesian update

posterior ∝ prior × treasury channel × shock/player channel × policy channel × inflation channel × activity anchor

Treasury and shock channels move the short horizon fastest. Policy and inflation matter more at the mid horizon. Activity anchors dominate ex-post validation and longer-horizon truth checks.

Player logic

Five-player Treasury game

  • Treasury: issuance mix, financing credibility, bill/coupon flexibility.
  • Federal Reserve: inflation credibility, easing delay, stability tools.
  • Sell side: inventory pressure, curve reshaping, flow amplification.
  • Buy side: duration allocation, inflation hedges, confirmation demand.
  • Foreign officials: reserve preservation, duration trimming, haven re-entry.
Version control

Version 1 is meant to evolve under a tracked roadmap

The framework is intentionally treated as a living research system. Changes are attached to the correct layer of the hierarchy rather than added as isolated patches. The public roadmap shows the current version, known limitations, and the next upgrade queue.
